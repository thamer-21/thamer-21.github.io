Bug reports

How to report a problem

Including these details up front usually cuts resolution time in half.

  1. Email amm540198@gmail.com with the subject line: [Bug] Asset Name — short description.
  2. Mention the asset version (e.g. v1.3.0) and your Unity version (e.g. 2022.3.15f1).
  3. Describe the steps to reproduce the issue — what you did, what you expected, what happened instead.
  4. Attach the Console error log or a screenshot/short video if possible.
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get an invoice for my purchase?
Purchases are handled entirely by the Unity Asset Store. You can download invoices from your Unity account under Order History.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds follow the Unity Asset Store refund policy. If an asset doesn't work as advertised, contact me first — most issues can be fixed quickly.
Do assets receive updates for new Unity versions?
Yes. All assets are tested against current LTS releases and updated when new Unity versions ship. Updates within the same major version are free.
Can I use an asset in multiple projects?
The Unity Asset Store license applies: one license per seat, usable across your own projects. See the Asset Store EULA for details.
Do you take custom tool requests?
Feature requests for existing assets are always welcome. For custom work, send an email with your requirements and I'll let you know if it's feasible.
